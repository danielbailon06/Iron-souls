Iron Souls

Iron Souls is a pixel art action RPG with a top-down perspective, inspired by classic retro exploration and combat games.

In a dark world filled with ruins, catacombs, and corrupted creatures, the player must fight their way through enemies, uncover hidden secrets, and face increasingly dangerous challenges. Each area introduces new threats, from cursed skeletons to fallen knights guarding ancient mysteries. Explore mysterious environments, improve your abilities, and survive in a hostile world where every battle matters.

With a retro visual style and a dark atmosphere, Iron Souls aims to combine the nostalgia of pixel art with a challenging and immersive experience.
